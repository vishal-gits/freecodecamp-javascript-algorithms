Cash Register

Cash Register is a digital register for cash transaction for any purchase.It accepts purchase price, cash paid(cash paid by buyer to seller), cash in drawer(cash with the buyer) as its input. Depending on the cash in drawer, it provides status and exact denominations of the cash(change) to be returned

  • Status: CASH COUNTER OPEN ,
    Change to be returned < cash counter balance
    Open for next transaction, Positive balance

  • Status: CASH COUNTER CLOSED ,
    Change to be returned = cash counter balance
    Closed for next transaction, Zero balance

  • Status: INSUFFICIENT FUNDS ,
    Change to be returned > cash counter balance
    OR specific currency not available to change
