Telephone Number Validator

This page will let you validate a US phone number.
In a typical use case scenario, The use may fill out a form field with any number, the validator can validate the format.

Below are a few examples of valid formats for US number.

  • 555-555-5555
  • (555)555-5555
  • (555) 555-5555
  • 555 555 5555
  • 5555555555
  • 1 555 555 5555
  • 1 456 789 4444

Below are a few examples of invalid formats for US number.

  • 123**&!!asdf#
  • 55555555
  • (6054756961)
  • 0 (757) 622-7382
  • -1 (757) 622-7382
  • 2 757 622-7382
  • 10 (757) 622-7382
  • 27576227382
  • 2(757)6227382
  • 555)-555-5555
  • (555-555-5555
  • (555)5(55?)-5555
  • 55 55-55-555-5
  • 11 555-555-5555
